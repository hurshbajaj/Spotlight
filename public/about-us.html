Spotlight

More about us

Spotlight is a sleek, all-in-one productivity platform built to help students take control of their time. It’s more than just a tool—it’s a focused, frictionless experience for organizing your academic life. Whether you're balancing classes, assignments, or extracurriculars, Spotlight keeps you on track with clarity and ease. Created by EXUN member Hursh Bajaj, Spotlight features seamless sign-in and sign-out flows, ensuring your workspace stays secure and personalized. At its core lies a highly interactive To-Do list—crafted for precision planning, real-time updates, and intuitive tracking of everything from daily tasks to long-term goals. The Timetable Manager offers a clean, visual overview of your weekly schedule—making it easy to map out classes, plan study blocks, and maintain balance. For collaboration, the Study Rooms page brings students together with built-in Google Meet links, allowing instant access to focused group sessions or peer discussions. From smart design to practical power, Spotlight is the ultimate digital study companion—designed to help you work smarter, not harder.
